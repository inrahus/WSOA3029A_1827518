Total Number of COVID Cases during March 2020 in South Africa

For this visualisation, I wanted to first test the ground with coding in JS and using D3. I opted for a simple display of data - as you can see, which is the total cases. I also limited this to the first month of COVID (March) as the statistics were already set/had set, so that I could more easily tackle the input of data.

I struggled greatly on the JS coding side, and although I have been able to grasp the basic display of the pie chart and given it a sense of readbility, I am unsatisfied with the lack of interaction and the colour scheme. Unfortunately, at this point, I am not adept enough with the fundtions of D3 to move the scheme from the D3 given presents to a more cohesive one that matches with the site aesthetic.
