this is vis.co

vis.co revolves around data visualisation, particularly in terms of COVID-19 related data, based in the South African context.

This site also features theoretical explorations of Data Visualisation and related coursework material.

For this site, I have decided to take a very visual and creative focused approach, and work on using and cohesively combining different colours and organising them appropriately throughout the site.
